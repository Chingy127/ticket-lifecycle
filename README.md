<h2>Lifecycle Stages</h2>

<p>
<img width="1399" height="756" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 3 37 37 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/10181315-3451-4310-8448-b5fde20d4ad0" />
</p>
<p>
The ticket lifecycle begins when a user submits a support request through the osTicket help desk portal. 
In this example, the user reports that the entire mobile and online banking system is down, which is a critical issue that needs immediate attention.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="819" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 3 46 17 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/917f6dc1-27e7-4d29-90d4-b2cb5c600ce7" />
</p>
<p>
Once the request is submitted, a ticket is automatically created in the system. 
The ticket contains important information such as the issue description, the user who submitted the request, and the time it was created.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="820" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 3 56 52 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/394a58fe-7040-428d-a205-bbc45755a913" />
<p>
A support manager assigns the ticket to a support agent who will investigate the issue. 
Agents can communicate within the ticket thread to coordinate who will handle the problem.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="827" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 3 58 39 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0fb5f197-0024-44bd-9ba1-d8b2adbe5aac" />
</p>
<p>
The support agent is given the appropriate access level so they can properly investigate the issue. 
In this example, the agent’s permissions are upgraded to allow full access to the necessary systems.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="577" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 04 14 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/afa58109-12d2-4b0f-9dd5-bace3c5aa83a" />
</p>
<p>
The ticket priority and Service Level Agreement (SLA) are updated to reflect the urgency of the issue. 
Because the banking system is down, the ticket is marked as an emergency and assigned a high-priority SLA.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1438" height="774" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 04 55 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bf5ee028-b56b-4808-a7a4-0ff044f39a72" />
</p>
<p>
Agents continue communicating and documenting their actions within the ticket thread while working toward resolving the issue. 
Once the problem is fixed, the ticket can be closed to complete the support process.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="758" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 09 35 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ecc65174-d7fd-4980-9006-e55d445cd94b" />
</p>
<p>
After reviewing the issue, the support agent escalates the ticket to the System Administrators department. 
Escalation is used when a problem requires a higher level of technical expertise to resolve.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="492" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 17 40 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d21d2d70-b993-4fe7-ae99-083ab2ebccd6" />
</p>
<p>
A system administrator investigates the issue and determines that the online banking backend server needs to be restarted due to configuration problems.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="501" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 19 25 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94987e15-a4fd-4590-a37c-3a3418823aea" />
</p>
<p>
The system administrator performs the restart and verifies that the banking system is functioning again. 
The update is documented in the ticket so that all agents can see the resolution steps.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="438" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-07 at 4 20 57 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aad2bcde-4027-489d-954f-31f11f7f655f" />
</p>
<p>
Once the issue is confirmed to be resolved with the user, the support agent closes the ticket. 
Closing the ticket marks the issue as completed in the help desk system.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1397" height="725" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-08 at 8 13 35 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/75314a54-ce4d-468e-aff8-1a73e4a46554" />
</p>
<p>
A second ticket is created to demonstrate another support scenario. 
In this case, users report that Adobe Reader is not working for multiple employees in the accounting department.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="729" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-08 at 8 25 23 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/71dfd77e-779e-40a5-9812-4804c1cb8155" />
</p>
<p>
The support agent investigates the issue and discovers that it is related to a recent software update. 
Possible solutions include installing the updated package manually or waiting for the update to deploy automatically.
</p>
<br />

<p>
<img width="1440" height="489" alt="Screenshot 2026-03-08 at 8 26 02 PM" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a721793f-d197-4006-b7a9-9ebc102789f9" />
</p>
<p>
After verifying that the updated software package installed successfully, the agent confirms that Adobe Reader is working again. 
The ticket is then closed to complete the support process.
</p>
<br />
